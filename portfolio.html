Pong V2

Context of the game

Our game known as "Pong V2" is a one player game and is
very simple to play. The directions are very simple as you just have to press
w to go up and s to do down. Also, you will have to reach 9 in order to win
the game. Our scoring system is if you can score against the bot. In order to
to do that you need to hit the ball past the paddle. The use of randomness of
in our game is due to the speed of the ball and where is goes when touching the paddle.
The game becomes more difficult as the points increase. For example, when the score
reaches 5, the backround changes and the ball changes as well. Including the speed of
the ball. The game can end when the player or the bot reaches a score of 9.
